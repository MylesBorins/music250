Music 250a

Physical Interaction Design for Music

Fall 2013

There will be one term project which will be presented or performed on Wednesday, December 4. The assignment is to invent and construct a musical physical interaction. The project should be done in groups of 3.

Consider:

  • Skill: who can use it?
  • LearningL how long will it take to learn?
  • Expression: can the user develop a model of the sound space and achieve a desired sound? is this important?
  • Improvisation: repeatable but instant response
  • Intuitiveness: is the mapping clear for the performer? for the audience?
