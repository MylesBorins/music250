Weekly homework and reading is intended to help your design process. To find a good idea, most of the time we need to have a lot of ideas.

As the quarter progresses and we start working on individual ideas, we will post those ideas to individual wikis. The idea behind this is that at the end of your project, you will already have some record of your design and build process and this will be invaluable as you document your work and share it with the world.
